About Me

Hi there! Thanks for stopping by my speaker portfolio. I love digging into new tech and turning that into content that's easy and fun to follow. I am a maker who loves the stage, and lately my talks have centered around AI - voice agents, chatbots and Azure Communication Services.

I also became a mum in 2025, which has been the happiest adventure of all. Now that I’m back from maternity leave, I’m excited to dive back into community events and start speaking again in 2026.
